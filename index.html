In this age of the Internet-of-Things, Sensorendipity empowers application creators to rapidly prototype and build innovative sensor-based solutions to problems by enabling easy access and real time visualisation of smartphone sensor readings, With this mobile framework on your phone, you create create a wide variety of applicataions with the help of mobile sensors.

Sensorendipity does not collect or share personal information, the sensor data is sent directly from the phone to the requesting device without any intermediates.

Be pleasantly surprised by sensors. That’s Sensorendipity.
