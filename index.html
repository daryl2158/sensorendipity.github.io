What is it ?

Sensorendipity is an open source framework that enables you to access the sensor data from your mobile device, easily and in real-time. By formatting the sensor data to be used live on any web-enabled platform, Sensorendipity now empowers you to discover the amazing world of web-enabled mobile device sensing. Use your mobile phone to control your lights. Use your smart-watch to monitor your exercise levels. Use your smart-eyewhere to detect your daily routine, or drive your car?! Use your mobile device everywhere, for anything, in any way you can dream of. Be amazed by your sensors. That's Sensorendipity.
